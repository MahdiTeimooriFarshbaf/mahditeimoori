About

Mahdi Teimoori is an Iranian Ney and Duduk player, composer and sound artist. His artistic practice combines Persian traditional music, electronic composition, sound design and live performance to create immersive musical experiences.

Projects

TARDID

A multidisciplinary project combining Ney, electronic music, ambient textures and sound design to explore uncertainty, silence and transformation.

Ney & Electronics

Live performances blending Persian wind instruments with modern electronic composition and looping.

Concerts and collaborations inspired by Middle Eastern, Persian and contemporary world music.
